How I work

Five years across renewable energy, consumer hardware, and live events.

I bring together mechanical engineering principles, hands-on prototyping, and sharp project coordination to turn ideas into functional, scalable outcomes.

Over the last five years I've led product and process design initiatives across renewable energy and consumer sectors, built roadmaps that carried concepts from ideation to implementation under tight constraints, and coordinated vendors, teams, and stakeholders for both technical engineering projects and large-scale community events. I apply CAD, Rhino/Grasshopper, and FEA tools to support design validation and iteration, and I'm currently growing Beauty in Balance, a creative network for multi-hyphenates balancing creative and professional lives.

What sets me apart is a systems mindset paired with adaptability. I thrive where complexity needs structure, sustainability is a priority, and design is treated as both function and narrative. I'm looking for roles in mechanical design, R&D, or project leadership at the intersection of sustainability and innovation.
